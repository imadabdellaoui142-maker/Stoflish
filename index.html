Our Story

More Than a Club

Founded in 2017, Stoflish has grown from a small study group into one of the most active student organizations at UABT.

A Home for English Enthusiasts

Stoflish Club was born from a simple idea: learning English shouldn't be boring. At the University of Abou Bekr Belkaïd Tlemcen, we created a space where students could practice, create, and grow together.

From intimate workshops to large-scale cultural events, we've built a community that celebrates language as a bridge to new worlds.

What We Do

Our Activities

We offer a diverse range of programs designed to make English learning engaging, practical, and fun.

01

Workshops & Training

Interactive sessions covering public speaking, creative writing, debate techniques, and interview preparation. Led by experienced mentors and guest speakers.

02

Debates & Discussions

Regular debate clubs and discussion circles where members tackle global issues, practice argumentation, and develop critical thinking in English.

03

Cultural Events

Movie nights, poetry slams, talent shows, and themed celebrations that bring language and culture together in unforgettable experiences.

04

Communication Labs

Safe spaces for students to practice speaking, receive constructive feedback, and build the confidence to communicate effectively.

05

Stoflish Talk

Our signature podcast and talk series featuring interviews, discussions, and storytelling by and for the student community.

06

E-Magazine Publishing

Curating, editing, and publishing digital magazines that showcase the best creative work from our talented student writers and artists.
